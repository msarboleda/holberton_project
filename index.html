Holberton School opens call to train developers in Medellín

Category: Opportunities

This software engineering academy founded in Silicon Valley comes to the city to train specialized talent in programming and Machine Learning, Virtual and Augmented Reality, Blockchain and Full-Stack web development.

The program has a duration of two years where tuition is free and students pay a percentage of their income, after graduates, if they get a job with a salary above three million pesos.

The curriculum is divided into:

  • Nine months of Full Stack studies.
  • Six months of internship (paid).
  • Nine months of specialization (Machine Learning, Web Stack Development, AR/VR, C Low level and Algorithms).

The academy's methodology is based on projects and teamwork. There are no "master classes" like a traditional university, but the learning spaces are open 24/7 and attendees can go at their own pace, fulfilling, of course, a compulsory academic load of 60 hours a week.

What are the costs of the program?

  • Before you start the program: $0
  • During the program: $0
  • If the graduate does not get a job: $0
  • If the graduate gets a job below $3 million per month: $0
  • If the graduate gets a job for more than $3 million per month: 17% of his/her salary for a period of 3.5 years.

Who is it aimed at?

To people who have an affinity for software development, regardless of their education or previous experience. To apply, you must:

  • Fill out an online form.
  • Complete small projects and online tests.
  • Create a website, from configuring a server to writing HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • Complete a technology challenge and questions on site or remote.
